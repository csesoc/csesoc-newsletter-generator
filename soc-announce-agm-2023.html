AGM Details

🎊 𝘼𝙉𝙉𝙐𝘼𝙇 𝙂𝙀𝙉𝙀𝙍𝘼𝙇 𝙈𝙀𝙀𝙏𝙄𝙉𝙂 🎊
CSESoc will be holding our Annual General Meeting for 2022 to report on the past year, vote on constitutional amendments and announce the election results for the 2023 CSESoc executive team!

There will be a proposed constitutional amendment to include 4 VP roles (namely VP External, VP Internal, and 2 VP Development) in our constitution (and others, which you can track via https://bit.ly/CSESocConstitution).

The following Executive positions will be available for election:
✨ Co-Presidents (2)
✨ Treasurer
✨ Secretary
✨ Arc Delegate
✨ GEDI Officer
🆕 Vice President External*
🆕 Vice President Internal*
🆕 Vice President Development* (2)
*Please note that the marked roles have not been officially added into the constitution as of yet, which will be done during AGM.

❓ 𝐌𝐎𝐑𝐄 𝐈𝐍𝐅𝐎𝐑𝐌𝐀𝐓𝐈𝐎𝐍 ❓
Full Duty Description: https://bit.ly/CSESocConstitution
Nomination procedure: https://bit.ly/ElectionNominationGuide

If you have any questions, please feel free to ask an Exec on the CSESoc Discord (bit.ly/CSESocDiscord) or email copresidents@csesoc.org.au.

🗳 𝐕𝐎𝐓𝐈𝐍𝐆 🗳
Once the election period starts, a voting link will be sent to your UNSW email, where you will be able to submit your votes for the 2023 executive team!

You will not have to register to vote. Everyone on our member list will be sent a voting form (ie, anyone in a CSE program).

📝 𝐀𝐆𝐄𝐍𝐃𝐀 📄
The agenda for the AGM is as follows:
- Apologies
- President's Report
- Treasurer’s Report
- Arc Delegate’s Report
- Reports from Directors
- Club's Constitution (Accepting or making amendments)
- Election of Executive
- General Business
- Business without notice

❌ 𝐈𝐌𝐏𝐎𝐑𝐓𝐀𝐍𝐓 𝐈𝐍𝐅𝐎𝐑𝐌𝐀𝐓𝐈𝐎𝐍 ❌
To ensure fair opportunity for candidates who deserve to be elected based on merit over popularity, there will be no campaigning permitted. This includes Facebook Events, Groups, Pages, physical material distributions, personally messaging to influence a vote in favour of a candidate, and any other form of publicity. Candidates who are found campaigning will have their nominations forfeited.
