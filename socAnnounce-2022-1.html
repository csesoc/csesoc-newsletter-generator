Enactus UNSW - CircEx - Apply Now
 
The student-led team at CircEx is looking for backend and React developers to help us during the two-week Industrial-Training and AHEGS-accredited Social Impact Traineeship (SIT).

CircEx is a new project at Enactus UNSW, where the team is building a chrome extension concept to digitalize second-hand shopping and reduce society's environmental footprint. When a user goes onto eBay or Amazon, the chrome extension would run a reverse image search on a charity op-shop database, finding second-hand alternatives for the items on the eCommerce site.

For software engineering students, CircEx is also approved to be used for your Industrial Training.

For more details, check out CircEx's concept video.
