CSESoc x Pearler Competition
✅𝗛𝗢𝗪 𝗜𝗧 𝗪𝗢𝗥𝗞𝗦✅
The CSESoc x Pearler Competition is a 'product' building competition! Central to Pearler's mission is to help people with their money and what better way than to use software engineering skills to achieve this purpose?

Your job in this competition is to build a **calculator** that can assist people in their savings, investment, or early retirement goals. Examples of relevant calculators include:

⏺Compound interest calculators - to determine how much interest they could earn by investing regularly
⏺FIRE (Financial Independence Retire Early) Calculators - to determine how much you need to have in savings and/or investments to retire by your target age
⏺Debt-free calculators - to determine how long it will take someone to pay off their debts.
