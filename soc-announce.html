TICKET SALES for the February 25th Imagination Hackathon is open.

UNIHACK is Australia’s largest student hackathon and this year we're inviting participants from across New Zealand and Australia. The event is open to students from TAFEs to PHDs across Australia and New Zealand and commences on the 25th of February 2022.

Inspired by the student hackathons in the United States and Europe, UNIHACK was created in 2014 by WIRED Monash.
