There's nothing better than watching your favourite movie under the stars! 🌌 Join us on Wednesday night of Week 2 for a moonlit cinema experience. 🌙📽🍿

🗓 Wednesday September 21st (Week 2)
🕕 6-9pm
📍 Library Lawn


🎟 TICKET INFO 🎟
Early Bird: $3.30
https://events.humanitix.com/csesoc-presents-cinema-after-dusk

General Attendance: $5 (pay on the day)

1. Shrek 🧌
2. Spirited Away 👻
