In 22T3, CSESoc will be running a new 🛠Open Dev Series🛠 initiative involving weekly sessions every Wednesday 2-4pm.

Over the course of the term, you'll learn how to build your own project with the popular MERN stack 📚 through workshops with interactive exercises and help sessions where experienced student developers 🧑‍💻 give you guidance on new coding exercises based on the workshops.

These workshops are beginner-friendly and students of all skill levels are welcome. 🤗 We encourage you to attend as many workshops as you can as it will form a complete set of skills to allow you to build your own projects.


Starting off in Week 1 is 📕HTML/CSS/JS📙 (the secret sauce behind all those pretty websites). The workshop will be held in Design Next Studio.

🌐 For the full timeline and details, visit: https://cseso.cc/opendev
